Hi, my name is Ian,

I recently graduated with a B.S. in computer science from NYU Shanghai and love programming gameplay, simulating nature, and experimenting with graphics. I like programming best when it makes art, and my passion projects often integrate my interest for 3D modeling and physical art such as acrylics and pen/pencil. I am Experienced with Unity, C#, and Javascript frameworks like P5 and Three, and dream of making video games for a living.


Thanks for visiting! This page is still in progress, so check back for updates...
